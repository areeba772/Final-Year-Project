How to Use Smart Cafe

1. Home Page

Start your journey on the home page. Here you'll find our latest offerings, discounts, and quick access to order your favorite meals.

2. Browse the Menu

Click on the Menu link in the navigation bar to see all available food items. You can browse through categories like Burgers, Pizzas, Drinks, and more.

3. Add to Cart

Found something you like? Click the Add to Cart button on the item card. You can select the quantity you want before adding it.

4. View Your Cart

Click the Cart icon at the top right of the screen to review your selected items. Here you can adjust quantities or remove items if needed.

5. Place Your Order

Once you're happy with your cart, proceed to checkout. Confirm your details and finalize the order. You'll receive an order confirmation immediately.

6. Track Your Orders

Go to My Orders to see the status of your current and past orders. You can track whether your food is being prepared or is ready for pickup.
